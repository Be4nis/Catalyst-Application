Reactions

Type of Reactions

I remember learning about the different types of reactions in grade 10 so I already understood this concept. However, the (metal and non-metal) activity series was a brand new concept. Even though this was a new concept it was easy to understand because we just had to look at the chart and then compare it with the reaction. Also, the in class labs were very fun and helped us visualize the reactions in real life.

Net Ionic Equations

Like any other new chemistry concept, I didn't understand it at first. I knew that they revealed the driving force behind an equation but I didn't understand how to determine the solubility of a compound or how it affected the reaction. Everyday I would practice some of the posted ionic compound sheets and overtime understood how to determine the net ionic equation of a reaction.
